Population density (2010) per country in the world

Data aquired from:
https://github.com/tillnagel/unfolding/blob/master/data/data/countries-population-density.csv



I chose for a simple pop menu, containing the text: "people per square kilometer", the country where the mouse is hovering at the moment and the raw data (rounded to integers). Background color and color of the water was chosen to better show the contrast between the data label with lowest population density and surrouding elements.
